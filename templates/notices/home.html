About SITDS

The School of Information Technology for Defence Systems (SITDS) at Defence Forces Technical College (DEFTEC), National Defence University - Kenya, is responsible for delivering high-quality education and training in information technology for defence applications. We prepare officers and technical personnel to design, deploy, and maintain secure and resilient IT systems for national defence.

Mission

To equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge to support defence IT systems and innovations.

How to use the Notice Board

  1. Admins register staff accounts (Senior Instructor, Adjutant, School Sergeant Major).
  2. Class Commanders manage students and class notices.
  3. Students view notices targeted to their class or public notices.
